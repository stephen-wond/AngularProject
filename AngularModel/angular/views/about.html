A LITTLE ABOUT DAVID

David Down (BSc Hons) is a fully qualified and insured practicing Sports Therapist based in Milton Keynes, Hertfordshire and London. He specialises in using the principles of sport and exercise to make sure that clients are training and competing in a safe and efficient manner. David firmly believes in the effective application of sports therapy and strength and conditioning techniques to improve a client’s entire fitness and sports performance goals.

David is well respected in his field, currently working at MK Dons Football Club as part of the Sports Medicine Department. David has also worked within a private practice setting at PC Physiotherapy in Milton Keynes where he has been able to treat a wide range of sportspeople from different disciplines. As a result of his diverse clinical experience, David has become proficient in many musculoskeletal modalities and offers treatment and rehabilitation to a wide variety of sports injuries and aims to restore clients to their pre injury state both quickly and safely.
